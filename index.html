Our Products

M-Sand (Manufactured Sand)

A crushed aggregate produced from hard rock stone, M-sand is a superior alternative to river sand. We ensure the highest quality through rigorous testing, including:

Testing includes

  • Sieve analysis
  • Silt content
  • Workability
  • Compressive strength tests

P-Sand (Plastering Sand)

A fine-grained, washed sand ideal for plastering applications, P-sand provides a smooth finish and minimizes cracking and shrinking.

Why P-Sand

  • Fine-grained, washed sand
  • Provides a smooth finish
  • Minimizes cracking and shrinking
